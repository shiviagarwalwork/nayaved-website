**Ancient Routine Meets Modern Schedule**

**5:30-6:00 AM - Brahma Muhurta (Creator's Time)**
- Why: Cortisol peaks, clarity highest
- Practice: Wake up, scrape tongue, drink warm water
- Modern hack: Start with gratitude, not phone

**6:00-8:00 AM - Morning Cleanse**
- Why: Kapha time, body eliminates waste
- Practice: Oil pulling, shower, light exercise
- Modern hack: Audio books while getting ready

**8:00-10:00 AM - Breakfast & Focus**
- Why: Metabolism warming up
- Practice: Warm breakfast, deep work
- Modern hack: No meetings before 10 AM

**10:00 AM-2:00 PM - Peak Performance (Pitta Time)**
- Why: Digestion & mental sharpness highest
- Practice: Biggest meal at noon, important decisions
- Modern hack: Schedule critical meetings 11 AM-1 PM

**2:00-6:00 PM - Afternoon Flow (Vata Time)**
- Why: Creative energy rises
- Practice: Creative work, light snack, movement
- Modern hack: Brainstorming sessions, walk meetings

**6:00-10:00 PM - Wind Down (Kapha Time)**
- Why: Body prepares for sleep
- Practice: Light dinner by 7 PM, no screens by 8 PM
- Modern hack: Reading, family time, baths

**10:00 PM-2:00 AM - Repair Time (Pitta Time)**
- Why: Body detoxes, liver regenerates
- Practice: Deep sleep
- Modern hack: Blackout curtains, sleep tracker

### **5. Ritucharya - Seasonal Living Guide**
**Why You Feel Different in Winter (And What to Do)**

**Season Breakdown:**

**Late Winter/Spring (Feb-May) - Kapha Season**
- What increases: Congestion, sluggishness, weight gain
- Why: Snow melts, accumulated Kapha liquefies
- Eat: Light, warm, spicy, bitter greens
- Avoid: Dairy, heavy foods, sleeping in
- Exercise: Vigorous (cardio, HIIT)
- Practice: Spring cleaning (body & home)
- Modern symptoms: Allergies, sinus issues, spring colds

**Summer (Jun-Aug) - Pitta Season**
- What increases: Heat, inflammation, irritability
- Why: External heat increases internal fire
- Eat: Cooling foods, sweet fruits, coconut, mint
- Avoid: Spicy, salty, alcohol, conflict
- Exercise: Swimming, gentle yoga, early morning
- Practice: Cooling breath work, nature time
- Modern symptoms: Skin rashes, acid reflux, anger

**Fall (Sep-Nov) - Vata Season**
- What increases: Dryness, anxiety, irregular digestion
- Why: Wind, change, transition
- Eat: Warm, moist, oily, grounding foods
- Avoid: Raw, cold, dry foods, travel
- Exercise: Grounding (yin yoga, walking)
- Practice: Oil massage, routine, consistency
- Modern symptoms: Anxiety spikes, insomnia, constipation

**Early Winter (Dec-Jan) - Kapha Building**
- What increases: Strength, immunity (if done right)
- Why: Body stores energy for cold
- Eat: Nourishing, warm, building foods
- Avoid: Excess cold exposure, fasting
- Exercise: Moderate, strength building
- Practice: Rest, nourish, hibernate wisely
- Modern symptoms: Seasonal depression (SAD), weight gain

**Interactive Calendar:** Enter your location, get seasonal guide

### **6. Fundamental Concepts Made Simple**

**Agni (Digestive Fire)**
- Western term: "Metabolism + Gut health"
- Signs of strong Agni: Regular hunger, good energy, clear skin
- Signs of weak Agni: Bloating, fatigue, brain fog
- How to strengthen: Ginger before meals, meal timing, avoid cold drinks

**Ojas (Vital Essence)**
- Western term: "Life force battery / Resilience"
- Signs of strong Ojas: Glowing skin, strong immunity, contentment
- Signs of depleted Ojas: Get sick often, exhausted, depressed
- How to build: Sleep, ghee, ashwagandha, reduce stress

**Ama (Toxins)**
- Western term: "Undigested food / Metabolic waste"
- Signs: White tongue coating, bad breath, sluggishness
- How to reduce: Fast intermittently, warm water, triphala

**Prana (Life Force)**
- Western term: "Breath + Energy + Vitality"
- How to increase: Breathwork, nature, clean food, meditation
